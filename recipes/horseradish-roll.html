Beef Horseradish Roll-up Recipe

How to make a Beef Horseradish Roll-up

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages fat-free cream cheese, softened
  • 3 ½ tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 12 (12 inch) flour tortillas
  • 30 spinach leaves, washed with stems removed
  • 1 ½ pounds thinly sliced cooked deli roast beef
  • 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese, horseradish, and Dijon mustard together in a bowl until combined.
  2. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese mixture over each tortilla; evenly arrange spinach leaves on top.
  3. Top with 2 slices roast beef, then divide and sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top.
  4. Gently roll up tortillas, starting at one end, into tight tubes. Wrap rolls with aluminum foil or plastic wrap to keep them tight. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
  5. Unwrap rolls as needed; slice into 2 or 3 pieces (or diagonally into 1-inch sections for appetizer portions).
