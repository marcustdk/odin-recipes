Pizza Recipe

This ultimate quick-start guide to homemade pizza includes an easy yeast dough, a simple no-cook sauce, and all the assembly steps for a perfect 12-inch pizza

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 1 cup (240ml) warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • active dry yeast or instant yeast (1 packet)
  • 3 cups (300g to 360g) all-purpose flour or bread flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce and Toppings

  • half cup tomato & onion mix or canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 and half cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • aSalt, dried oregano, and your favorite meats or veggies

Steps

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a large bowl, whisk the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Make the Dough: Add the flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a soft dough forms. Knead on a lightly floured surface for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let it Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a damp towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 to 1.5 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the Oven: Turn your oven to 450 F or 230 C. If you have a pizza stone, leave it inside while it preheats.
  5. Shape & Top: Punch down the dough and stretch or roll it into a 12-inch circle. Brush lightly with olive oil, then spread your sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  6. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
